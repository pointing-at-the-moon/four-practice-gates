# The Practice of Giving
## 《施——從巴利之根到法界供養》

**Volume 2 of [The Four Practice Gates](../README.md)** — the gate of outward action.

*DĀNA — From Pāli Roots to the Dharmadhātu*

A companion to [*Mindful of the Buddha*](../nian/) (Volume 1). Where NIAN establishes continuous awareness, DĀNA loosens the grip of "mine" through the practice of giving.

- **Author**: 釋慧鏡 (Shi Huijing)

### Planned Structure

Nine parts, ~30 papers:

- **Part 1** The semantics and roots of dāna
- **Part 2** Dāna in the Āgamas
- **Part 3** The Vinaya and Abhidharma on giving
- **Part 4** The early Mahāyāna turn to dāna-pāramitā
- **Part 5** Prajñā literature: giving without abiding
- **Part 6** The Śūraṅgama's reverse dāna thesis
- **Part 7** The Lotus Sūtra: the gift of body and life
- **Part 8** The Avataṃsaka: dharmadhātu offering
- **Part 9** Contemporary diagnosis and AI coda
