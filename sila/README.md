# From Pāli Vinaya to the Sign-less Precepts
## 《戒——從巴利律到無相戒》

**Volume 3 of [The Four Practice Gates](../README.md)** — the gate of the boundary of action.

*SĪLA — From the Pāli Vinaya to the Sign-less Precepts*

A companion to [*Mindful of the Buddha*](../nian/) (Volume 1) and [*The Practice of Giving*](../dana/) (Volume 2). Where NIAN establishes continuous awareness and DĀNA loosens the grip of "mine" through outward action, SĪLA returns action to its own structure — character (*sīla*), sense-restraint (*saṃvara*), and the casuistic textual system (*vinaya*).

### Planned Structure

Nine parts, ~29 papers:

- **Part I** Pāli Roots — *sīla* / *saṃvara* / *vinaya* foundations
- **Part II** The Āgama View — the three ethical limbs, *pañca-sīla*, *uposatha*, *sīla-gandha*
- **Part III** Vinaya and the Three Schools — Pāli Theravāda / Dharmaguptaka / Mūlasarvāstivāda comparative reading
- **Part IV** The Mahāyāna Turn — three-body bodhisattva *sīla* · Nāgārjuna on *śīla-pāramitā* · *Bodhisattvabhūmi* / *Yingluo* receiving-architecture
- **Part V** The 戒體 Question — Daoxuan / Zhiyi / Yogācāra three-school dialogue on the nature of precept-substance (East Asian doctrinal innovation)
- **Part VI** Bhaiṣajyaguru: Support Conditions for *Sīla* — the twelve vows as material and institutional ground for ethical conduct
- **Part VII** Prajñā: Sign-less Precepts — Diamond Sūtra / Vimalakīrti / three-circle emptiness applied to *sīla*
- **Part VIII** Śūraṅgama, Lotus, Huayan: The Complete Precept — four pure precepts → DHYĀNA handoff
- **Part IX** Modern Diagnosis and Living Sīla — therapeutic anxiety vs legalism · *sīla* of speech in the age of AI · *sīla* as daily art
